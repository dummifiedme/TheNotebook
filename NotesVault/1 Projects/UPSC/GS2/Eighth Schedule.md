## Eighth Schedule
It deals with the 22 official languages recognized by the Indian Constitution:

*   Assamese
*   Bengali
*   Bodo
*   Dogri (Dongri)
*   Gujarati
*   Hindi
*   Kannada
*   Kashmiri
*   Konkani
*   Mathili (Maithili)
*   Malayalam
*   Manipuri
*   Marathi
*   Nepali
*   Oriya
*   Punjabi
*   Sanskrit
*   Santhali
*   Sindhi
*   Tamil
*   Telugu
*   Urdu
